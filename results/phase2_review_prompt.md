- 4 소재: Pt/HfO<sub>x</sub>/Ti(25×), Ferroelectric(333×), Metallic Nanowire(1.97×), TaO<sub>x</sub>/Ta(146×)

  3. 9% 확률 catastrophic degradation 관찰
